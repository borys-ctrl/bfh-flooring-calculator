⏱️ How long does it take?

Most estimates take 15–30 minutes. Larger properties (over 1,500 sq. ft.), unusual layouts, or lots of questions can take longer. Since estimators run a back-to-back schedule, we recommend visiting our showroom first — our consultants there aren't on the clock and can answer questions in depth.

How to prepare for your estimate

  • Be at the property at the scheduled date and time (unless you've arranged otherwise).
  • Let everyone know the estimator is coming.
  • Decide on the areas to be measured ahead of time.
  • Make areas accessible — open doors, clear space for measuring.
  • Check condo rules with your property manager for any requirements or restrictions.
  • Visit our showroom first to see materials and get your questions answered.

What to expect: 5 simple steps

  1. Book your estimate. Call or text (808) 210-4807, or request a callback via the chat (lower-right corner) or our contact page. We'll call during business hours.
  2. Get confirmation & reminders. You'll receive a confirmation email plus a reminder on the day of your estimate.
  3. Meet your estimator. They'll ask about your material, furniture, color choice, soundproofing needs, and timeline, then measure the areas and flag any potential issues.
  4. Receive your quote. In most cases you'll get an itemized quote by email the same or next day. Our team follows up to make sure it arrived.
  5. Make your decision. Accept in full, request changes, buy materials only, or order labor only — whatever works best for you.
💡 Pro tip: Let us know the best way to enter your property or where to park — especially in Waikiki and other areas with limited parking.

Frequently asked questions

Can you give me a quote over the phone from my square footage?

Not accurately. Our estimator looks at far more than area — property access, job complexity, and any extra services all affect the price. An in-person estimate is the only way to give you a number you can truly rely on — and it's free.

Can you quote based on another company's estimate?

We can't, because we don't know exactly what labor and materials they included. Our scope and pricing can differ significantly, so we quote only after our own estimate.

Can you quote without seeing some areas of my property?

No — we quote only after visually inspecting every area to be quoted. This protects you from surprises later on.

I have an identical unit you already measured — can I reuse that quote?

Each property needs its own visit and quote, even when units look identical, since conditions and access can vary.

Is the estimate really free, even far from your showroom?

Yes — estimates are free anywhere on Oahu, with no travel charge.

What goes into the cost of a flooring installation?

Every project is a little different, but the final price almost always comes down to a handful of key factors. Understanding them up front helps you compare quotes fairly and avoid surprises.

🧱 Material you choose

The flooring itself is usually the biggest line item. Vinyl plank and laminate sit on the affordable end, while solid hardwood and premium tile cost more per square foot.

📐 Square footage

Larger areas cost more in total but often less per square foot. Small rooms with lots of cuts and corners can take more labor per foot.

🛠️ Subfloor prep

If the existing subfloor needs leveling, moisture sealing, or old flooring removed, that adds labor and materials to the job.

🚪 Layout & details

Stairs, transitions, trim, thresholds, and intricate patterns all add time and skill, which affects the installed price.

What to expect during your visit

When you arrive, a flooring consultant will ask a few quick questions to understand your project:

  • Which material interests you — vinyl plank, hardwood, or laminate?
  • What flooring features matter most to you?
  • What colors do you prefer?
  • What's your budget for materials?
  • Which areas of your property are you updating?

From there, they'll show you options that fit your needs, check availability and pricing, and help you take home samples to test. Most materials are in stock and ready to order.

💳 Pay by cash, personal check, cashier's check, debit, or credit card. If you're buying materials only and paying by personal check, we release the flooring once the check clears.

💡 Pro tip: Always take free samples home. Place them near your walls, furniture, and countertops and view them at different times of day — the same floor can look very different depending on lighting, angle, and room.
💡 Pro tip: When you order, don't forget the accessories — baseboards, stair noses, T-moldings, end caps, and underlayment.

How to prepare for your visit

It's not required, but a little prep helps you make the best choice:

  1. Decide what you like. Notice flooring at friends' and neighbors' homes — color, type, and feel underfoot. Snap a photo of what you like and note what you don't.
  2. Browse our store & blog. Our online store shows what's in stock in Hawaii, and our blog is a deep resource of flooring guidance for homeowners.
  3. Find inspiration online. Save photos of floors you love and show our consultants — they'll do their best to match the look.
